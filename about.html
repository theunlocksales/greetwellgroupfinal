Our Story

A Journey of Excellence in
Agricultural Exports

GreetWell Group was founded with a singular vision: to bridge the gap between India's rich agricultural heritage and the global marketplace. What began as a passion for promoting India's finest produce has evolved into a mission to deliver excellence, sustainability, and progress to businesses worldwide.

Our journey started with a deep understanding of India's agricultural landscape and a commitment to ethical farming practices. We recognized that quality doesn't just happen—it's cultivated through dedication, expertise, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. Today, we stand as a testament to what's possible when passion meets purpose.

Every product we export carries with it the story of the farmers who nurtured it, the quality control experts who perfected it, and our team's dedication to delivering nothing but the best. We don't just export agricultural products; we export trust, quality, and the promise of progress.

Our Journey

The GreetWell Story

Milestones that shaped our journey to excellence

2015

Foundation & Vision

GreetWell Group was established with a vision to export India's finest agricultural products globally. Started with direct partnerships with local farmers in Maharashtra and Madhya Pradesh.

First export shipment to UAE
2017

Expansion & Growth

Expanded operations to 5 countries and established partnerships with 100+ farmers. Introduced premium turmeric exports with high curcumin content, gaining recognition in international markets.

ISO certification achieved
2019

Quality Certification

Achieved international quality certifications and organic certifications. Launched state-of-the-art processing and packaging facility ensuring premium quality standards.

Organic certification obtained
2021

Global Recognition

Reached 30+ countries worldwide with consistent export volumes. Received industry recognition for quality excellence and sustainable farming practices. Expanded product range significantly.

Excellence award received
2023

Technology Integration

Implemented advanced technology for quality control, traceability, and logistics management. Introduced cold chain logistics ensuring maximum freshness during transportation.

Advanced tech implementation
2025

Present & Beyond

Today, GreetWell Group serves 50+ countries with 200+ satisfied clients. We continue to innovate, expand, and set new benchmarks in agricultural exports while staying true to our core values.
