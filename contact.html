FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and processes

What is your minimum order quantity?

+
Our minimum order quantity varies by product. For most agricultural products, we can accommodate orders starting from a 20-foot container. For specific requirements, please contact us for customized solutions.

How long does shipping typically take?

+
Shipping time depends on your destination. Generally, sea freight takes 15-30 days depending on the port, while air freight takes 5-7 days. We provide real-time tracking for all shipments.

Do you provide organic certifications?

+
Yes, we provide various certifications including Organic (NPOP & NOP), ISO 9001:2015, FSSAI, and other certifications as required by your country's import regulations.

Can you handle custom packaging and labeling?

+
Absolutely! We offer complete custom packaging and private labeling services. You can use your own brand name, logo, and packaging design to meet your market requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

+
We accept various payment methods including Letter of Credit (LC), Telegraphic Transfer (TT), and other internationally accepted payment terms. Payment terms are flexible and can be discussed based on order size.

Do you provide product samples?

+
Yes, we can provide samples for quality evaluation. Sample costs and shipping charges may apply, which can be adjusted against your first bulk order. Contact us to request samples.
